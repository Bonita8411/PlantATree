Acer palmatum Bloodgood Red Japanese Maple
Highly ornamental deciduous tree with stunning red-purple foliage. A small neat upright tree, with highly ornamental partially divided, serrated edged foliage of intense colour. Around 3 m tall and wide. In Autumn the foliage becomes bright crimson red before falling. In cold climates may form attractive red fruits also. Grows in most free draining garden soils in full or partial sun. Best in organic rich soils. Cold and frost tolerant. Excellent feature free suitable for smaller gardens.
